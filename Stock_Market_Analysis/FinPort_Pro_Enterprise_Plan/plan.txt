================================================================================
 PROJECT OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Platform Name:    FinPort Pro — Tactical Portfolio Management Platform
Business Goal:    Replace Excel + Power BI with scalable, event-driven system
Architecture:     Microservices + Event-Driven (Kafka) + Angular SPA
Scale Target:     5+ million users, horizontal scaling
Full Implementation: 8-9 Months (16-18 Sprints)

================================================================================
 THREE-LAYER ARCHITECTURE
================================================================================

The system is segregated into three independent, loosely-coupled layers:

  LAYER 1 — UI (Angular)
    - Plan File: plan_ui_layer.txt
  
  LAYER 2 — Backend (Spring Boot Microservices)
    - Plan File: plan_backend_layer.txt
    - Details: plan_backend_services.txt (Detailed Structures)

  LAYER 3 — Database & Data Architecture
    - Plan File: plan_database_layer.txt

================================================================================
 IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP (8-9 MONTHS)
================================================================================

  The detailed sprint-by-sprint breakdown is split into two parts:

  Part 1: plan_implementation_roadmap.txt (Months 1-4)
    - Phases 1-4: Foundation, Core Backend, UI, Risk Engine.
    - Local bare-metal development focus.

  Part 2: plan_implementation_roadmap_part2.txt (Months 5-9)
    - Phases 5-8: AI, Hardening, Stabilization, Enterprise Automation.
    - Docker, Kubernetes, and CI/CD automation finalized at the end.
